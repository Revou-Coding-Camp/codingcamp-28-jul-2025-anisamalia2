About Us

Lupy Florist is one of the most well-known florists in the Special Region of Yogyakarta. With a feminine and cheerful concept expressed through the choice of flower colors and arrangement styles, Lupy Florist brings joy and elegance to every floral creation

Lupy Florist offers a wide variety of fresh cut flowers, both locally grown and imported. They also provide various types of floral arrangements, including Bouquets, Hand-tied Wedding Bouquets, Bloom Boxes, Vase Arrangements, Standing Flowers, Money Bouquets, and more. Lupy Florist offers a wide selection of floral arrangements perfect for complementing every special occasion in your life.

The slogan "Feel The Bloom" is an invitation from Lupy Florist for everyone to experience the blossoming of flowers, symbolizing happiness and love—by anyone, anywhere, and anytime. With our commitment to excellent products and services, we hope every customer can feel the beauty of each bloom, even spreading that joy to others who simply see it.
